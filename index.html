Why LibraryFinder?

Welcome to LibraryFinder, your ultimate companion for navigating the wealth of resources at UW-Madison libraries! We understand that juggling coursework, research, and campus life can be overwhelming. That’s why we’ve made it our mission to simplify the way our fellow peers and faculty connect with the libraries we rely on.

LibraryFinder was created to provide quick and seamless access to essential library information, from locations and hours to upcoming events and available collections. No more searching through multiple websites or struggling to find the right library for your study needs—we’ve streamlined it all into one user-friendly platform.

  • Convenience at Your Fingertips: Find the nearest library, check its hours, or see what’s happening there—all in just a few clicks.
  • Stay Informed: Discover upcoming events, workshops, and activities hosted by UW-Madison libraries.
  • Effortless Resource Access: Search for books, research materials, and other resources with ease, so you can focus on what matters most.
  • Tailored for UW-Madison: Built specifically for us students, faculty, and staff, LibraryFinder is designed with your campus needs in mind.

At LibraryFinder, we’re passionate about making academic life easier and more productive. Whether you’re studying for finals, conducting groundbreaking research, or just looking for a quiet corner to read, we’re here to help you make the most of UW-Madison’s incredible library system.

Explore. Discover. Achieve.

With LibraryFinder, the power of knowledge is always within reach.
